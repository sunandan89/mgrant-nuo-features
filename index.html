Core Modules

Detailed walkthrough of every functional module available to NGO users on the platform.

🏢 NGO Onboarding & Profile Management ▶

NGOs receive a secure self-registration link from the donor SPOC. Upon registration, they set up credentials and complete their organisational profile — including legal registrations, bank details, leadership information, and operational geography.

  • Self-Registration Portal: Secure invite-based signup with Google or Office 365 SSO
  • Organisation Profile: PAN, 12A/80G certificates, audited financials, governance structure
  • NGO Dashboard: Ongoing projects, proposals in progress, funding received, and profile status — all in one view
  • Multi-User Access: Create additional users with role-based permissions (PM, Finance, M&E)
  • Repeat NGO Prefill: Returning NGOs get pre-populated profiles — no duplicate data entry
🔍 Due Diligence & Compliance ▶

A structured workflow for capturing, validating, and storing NGO compliance and governance information. Donors review and rate each NGO before activating grants.

  • Document Upload: Registration certificates, audited financials, compliance reports, governance policies
  • Status Tracking: Pending Review → Conducted / Waived Off / Not Conducted
  • Rating System: Configurable rating scale assigned by donor reviewers
  • History & Audit Log: All due diligence outcomes logged in the NGO's profile history
  • Automated Notifications: NGO receives email on status change; Admin dashboard filterable by DD status
📝 Proposals, RFPs & Grant Application Forms ▶

The complete proposal lifecycle — from discovering relevant RFPs to submitting structured Grant Application Forms (GAFs) with technical, financial, and compliance details.

  • RFP Scouting Agent: NEW AI agent that understands your organisation's focus areas and proactively surfaces relevant RFPs and funding opportunities
  • GAF Submission: Structured application forms with configurable questions, budget formats, and selection criteria
  • Open & Closed Modes: Public proposals via portal or private invitations to specific NGOs
  • Multi-Level Assessment: Commenting, clarification requests, scoring rubrics, and role-based reviewer access
  • Board Note Auto-Generation: System auto-generates Board Notes post-approval, editable by Grant Manager / PL / CEO
  • Financial Quarters: Budget proposals tied to fixed Indian fiscal quarters (Apr–Jun, Jul–Sep, Oct–Dec, Jan–Mar)
📁 Project Setup & Management ▶

The heart of mGrant NUO. Every approved grant becomes a Project — with 11 detailed tabs covering identity, geography, budgets, targets, tasks, and results.

  • Auto Project Code: Unique project code generated upon Board Note approval
  • Details Tab: Implementation type, project name, dates, frequency, portfolio themes, SDG alignment, MoU upload
  • Entity (Donor) Linking: Select donor → auto-populate contact, email, and PoC details
  • Geographical Mapping: Hierarchical selection — State → District → Block — with visual overview panel
  • MoU & Sanction Letters: Auto-generated, routed for approval, signed versions stored in the system
  • Sub-Implementation Support: For projects where partner NGOs carry out ground-level work
💵 Budgeting & Fund Management ▶

End-to-end financial management — from committed budgets and fund requests to disbursements, utilisation tracking, and reconciliation.

  • Budget Allocation: Break down sanctioned amount by Budget Heads (Personnel, Travel, Programme costs, etc.)
  • Fund Receipts & Tranches: Record planned instalments and actual payments received — with real-time summary cards
  • Fund Requests: NGOs raise requests against quarterly ceilings; approval workflows based on configurable thresholds
  • Disbursement Tracking: Auto-update released vs. requested amounts; UTR number recording
  • Unutilised Fund Handling: Under 1 year: no-cost extension. Multi-year: rollover of unutilised disbursed funds
  • Partner Payments: For sub-implementation projects — plan and record payments to partner NGOs separately
  • Instalment & Milestone Reminders: Automated alerts for upcoming disbursement and reporting deadlines
📊 Project Monitoring & Reporting ▶

Structured monitoring across progress, finance, and impact — with quarterly cycles, field visit reports, and LFA tracking.

  • QPR (Quarterly Progress Reports): Submit intervention targets vs. actual achievements + narrative report
  • QFR (Quarterly Fund Utilisation): Utilisation statements + reconciliation reports submitted quarterly
  • QRR (Quarterly Review Report): Auto-generated summary combining QPR + QFR; editable by Grant Manager
  • Field Visit Reports: Grant Managers submit within 10 days of a site visit
  • LFA Tracking: Define and monitor Outputs, Outcomes, and Impact indicators at the project level
  • Granular Data Capture: Detailed KPI / beneficiary-level data for impact assessment (integrates with mForm)
  • Output & Outcome Tables: Cross-project tracking with filters by Project, Financial Year, Focus Area, Partner, State
✅ Task Management ▶

Create, assign, and track work items across all projects — field visits, report deadlines, procurement, milestones, or any activity that needs monitoring.

  • Task Board: View all tasks with priority (Low / Medium / High), status (Todo / In Progress / Done), and due dates
  • Auto Closure Status: System flags overdue tasks as "Delayed" automatically
  • Quick Status Update: Change status directly from the list view — no need to open the full record
  • Cross-Project View: See all tasks across all projects in a unified list
📋 Approval Workflows ▶

Centralised approval tracker for budget changes, project updates, report submissions, and any record requiring sign-off.

  • Dashboard Counters: Total Requests, Approved, Pending on Me, Rejected — at a glance
  • Multi-Level Workflows: Configurable approval chains (e.g., Proposal → Programme Officer → Donor Review)
  • Module-Wise Filtering: Filter by Budget Utilisation, Project, Report, etc.
  • Auto-Notifications: Submitter notified on approval/rejection; escalation alerts for overdue approvals
  • No-Code Configuration: Administrators can modify approval workflows without developer involvement
🔄 Project Renewal & Extension ▶

Multi-year project management with structured renewal cycles and extension mechanisms.

  • Annual Renewal: Each year treated as a new cycle — fresh GAF, new sanction letter post-approval
  • No-Cost Extension: System generates editable extension letters routed for approvals
  • Extension with New MoU: Formal extension with fresh documentation and sanction letters
  • Automated Notifications: GM, PL, CEO, assessor, and NGO notified at every step

AI-Powered Intelligence NEW

mGrant NUO leverages the Model Context Protocol (MCP) to bring AI directly into your grant management workflow.

💬

Natural Language Data Chat

Ask questions like "What is the total unutilised amount across all active grants?" or "Which projects have overdue QPR submissions?" — and get instant, accurate answers from your live data.

  • 34 MCP tools across 7 functional categories
  • Real-time budget and fund availability calculations
  • KPI tracking and quarterly utilisation queries
🔎

RFP Scouting Agent

An AI agent that continuously scans for relevant RFPs and funding opportunities based on your organisation's thematic areas, geography, and track record.

  • Understands your NGO's focus areas and capabilities
  • Proactively surfaces matching proposals
  • Reduces time spent on manual opportunity discovery
📄

AI Report Summarisation

Automatically extract key findings from lengthy qualitative reports. Identify patterns across narrative submissions and transform them into actionable insights.

  • Theme identification across multiple reports
  • Insight generation from qualitative data
  • Executive summaries auto-generated
📝

Proposal Writing Assistance

Accelerate proposal drafting with AI-assisted writing. Access past successful proposals, get content recommendations, and ensure alignment with donor requirements.

  • Template management for different donor formats
  • Content recommendations from past successes
  • Donor requirement alignment checks
